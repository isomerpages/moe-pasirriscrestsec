<p>Dear Crestians and Parents/Guardians of the 2025 Graduating Cohort</p>
<p><strong>Information on Results Collection</strong>
<br>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The results of the 2025 Singapore-Cambridge
GCE O-Level Examinations will be released on <strong>Wednesday, 14 January 2026</strong>,
at <strong>Pasir Ris Crest Secondary School</strong>. We congratulate your
child/ward for completing this important milestone and affirm you for your
continuous support seeing them through their education journey together
with us.
<br>
<br><strong>Administrative Instructions on Results Release for School Candidates</strong>
<br>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; School candidates are to report to the <strong>School Hall</strong> at <strong>1.45pm</strong> to
collect their results. Parents/guardians are welcome to accompany their
child/ward to collect their results.
<br>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;School candidates are reminded
to be properly groomed and dressed in their school attire. Coloured hair,
multiple earrings and non-school uniform are not allowed.
<br>
<br><strong>Proxy Collection</strong>
<br>4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; School candidates who are unable to collect
their results in person should inform the school before 14 January through
your Home Tutor, and can appoint a proxy to collect a physical copy of
their results on their behalf.
<br>5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Proxies can proceed to the General Office
to collect the results.&nbsp; Proxies are required to produce the relevant
documents (Photo ID for identification &amp; Note of Authorisation) for
the school’s verification when collecting the results.
<br>6.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; School candidates may contact the school
through your Home Tutors for assistance if candidates are unable to appoint
a proxy. Please do so before 14 January.</p>
